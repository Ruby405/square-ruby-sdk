## Tender Cash Details

Represents the details of a tender with `type` `CASH`.

### Structure

`TenderCashDetails`

### Fields

| Name | Type | Tags | Description |
|  --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `buyer_tendered_money` | [`Money Hash`](/doc/models/money.md) | Optional | Represents an amount of money.<br><br>__Important:__ Unlike version 1 of the Connect API, __all monetary amounts<br>returned by v2 endpoints are positive.__ (In v1, monetary amounts are negative<br>if they represent money being paid _by_ a merchant, instead of money being<br>paid _to_ a merchant.) |
| `change_back_money` | [`Money Hash`](/doc/models/money.md) | Optional | Represents an amount of money.<br><br>__Important:__ Unlike version 1 of the Connect API, __all monetary amounts<br>returned by v2 endpoints are positive.__ (In v1, monetary amounts are negative<br>if they represent money being paid _by_ a merchant, instead of money being<br>paid _to_ a merchant.) |
